Research Interests

My research interests broadly lie in the areas of reinforcement learning, Stochastic approximation, and optimization. Currently, my research focuses on provably efficient reinforcement learning algorithms for Markovian and non-Markovian systems, as well as on convergence guarantees for first- and second-order optimization algorithms.
